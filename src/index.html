First Edition of the E-Olympics is FINALLY HAPPENING

Opening ceremony: 12/31/2022 - 15:00 GMT

  • What is the E-Olympics?

    The E-Olympics is an international E-Sports championship. People from all over the world play their favorite games, representing their homeland, in the hope of reaching the first place.

  • Who can participate?

    Anyone! As long as you are subscribed, you will be called to every event related to the game you chose.

  • What about the teams?

    The teams will be arranged by the staff based on the nationality. The teams, though, won't be static: people will be eliminated from the event accordingly to their performance and the teams will be rearranged. This creates the possibility of meeting new cool people!
